# Hydrogeological Characterization of the Actopan Valley Aquifer

## Overview

This repository documents an academic research project focused on the hydrogeological characterization of the Actopan Valley Aquifer (Veracruz, Mexico).

The study integrates geophysical surveys, hydrochemical measurements and GIS-based spatial analysis to evaluate groundwater conditions and identify areas potentially affected by saline intrusion.

---

## Objectives

- Characterize the hydrogeological conditions of the aquifer.
- Acquire field geophysical data using Vertical Electrical Soundings (VES).
- Analyze groundwater electrical conductivity.
- Integrate spatial information using GIS.
- Produce thematic maps supporting groundwater interpretation.

---

## Field Methods

The field campaign included:

- Vertical Electrical Soundings (Schlumberger array)
- Magnetometry
- Groundwater conductivity measurements
- Sediment sampling
- Bathymetric survey
- Static water level measurements
